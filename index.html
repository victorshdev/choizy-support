Welcome to the support page for RandiGo.app - the app that helps you make decisions when you can't decide!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new list?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter a name for your list, and start adding items.

How do I make a random selection?

Open a list and tap the "Choose Randomly" button at the bottom of the screen.

Can I share my selection results?

Yes! After a selection is made, tap the "Share" button to send your result via message, email, or social media.

How do I edit a list?

Open the list you want to modify and tap the "Edit" button in the top right corner.
