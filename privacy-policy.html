Privacy Policy for Choizy

Last updated: May 4, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for choosing Choizy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how it is used, and what rights you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Information Stored on Your Device

Choizy stores the lists and items you create locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains private on your device.

Automatically Collected Data

Our app integrates with Google AdMob for displaying advertisements and may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Coarse location data (general area, not precise location)
  • Usage data (such as app interactions and viewed advertisements)
  • Performance and crash data to improve app stability

This data collection is used for showing relevant advertisements and improving app performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Providing and maintaining the functionality of the app
  • Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analyzing app performance and fixing bugs
  • Improving the user experience

We do not use your data for any other purposes beyond what is described in this policy.

Third-Party Service Providers

We work with the following third parties who may collect or receive data:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements in the app. You can learn more about how Google uses data at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
  • Apple: For analytics through App Store Connect.

These third parties process your data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since most app data is stored locally on your device, the security of this data is managed by your device's operating system.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
